Our History

40 YEARS OF EXPERIENCE

Established and named by her mother Mohtarma Aisha Begam, Glam & Glow Beauty Parlour was launched in 1982 by Farhat Ehsan and her husband Sheikh Ehsan as a small beauty salon in Karachi. Today, as we mark the 40th year of our operations in Pakistan, we stand tall as one of the leading brands in the beauty industry of Pakistan. Our transition from one single branch primarily offering bridal makeup services, to one of the fastest growing network of modern and full-service Beauty and Hair salons in Pakistan is earmarked with a vision to deliver experiences that not only move hearts and minds but also fuels passion for progress and growth.

Our clients are most dear to us and the relationships we have made with a diversified portfolio of clients from different cross-sections and socioeconomic groups are by far the biggest assets we have made over the years. Client satisfaction lies at the core of our services. Our staff is professionally trained and skilled not only at the art of the services they specialize in, but also in providing their clients the comfort and peace they are looking for when they enter our salons. The services and the facilities across all our branches complement the same hygienic and relaxing atmosphere that our clients experience whenever they walk into any of our branches.
